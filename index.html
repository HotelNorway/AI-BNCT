Key Integration Mechanisms:
  • Closed-Loop Validation: AI predictions → Synthesis → Radiolabeling → Biological testing → Results retrain AI (6-9 month cycles)
  • Co-Design Workshops: Semi-annual meetings where all teams jointly review candidates, experimental results, and equity implications
  • Cross-Disciplinary Training: Researchers rotate between labs for hands-on exposure to complementary methodologies
  • Stakeholder Engagement: Regular consultations with patient groups (including rare cancer communities), clinicians, and regulatory agencies throughout project lifecycle
  • EDI Integration: Cancer type representation analysis ensures balanced focus on rare and common cancers; cell line diversity panels validate carriers across ethnic backgrounds
